About the Photographer

Primrose Rosenthal

Greetings! I’m Primrose Rosenthal, a passionate photographer with expertise in portraits, group portraits, and products. I love capturing the uniqueness and emotions of people through stunning pictures, primarily through cosplay photography! I enjoy bringing beloved characters to life and celebrating the dedication cosplayers put into their work. I also enjoy showcasing the dynamics and connections between individuals in group photography alongside the intricacies and beauty of products. Let’s create something incredible together!
